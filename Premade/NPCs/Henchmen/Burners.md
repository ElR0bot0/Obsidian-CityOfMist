#borderliner  #henchmen 
Where [[Spearheads|spearheads]] fail burners come to take control and achieve Barton's goals. This group of combat expert borderliners are normally issued as leaders to spearheads or, for more challenging threats, as a team consisting only of others like them.

They are named like that for a reason. When a burner finishes their mission their psyche breaks because of  the powers of the Spear and succumbs to the siren of hyperviolence.

On rare occasions, a burner may manage to endure the maelstrom of voices and single down one in particular which then becomes their mythos. Such are the cases of  [[Jane Radcliff]], an aggressive knife-fighter from the Industrial Zone and [[Belay Theron]], a South African mercenary. Unlike the rest of the people in the operation this two could remember Barton's face ant that is why he only communicates with them via visions or his network of spearheads.
