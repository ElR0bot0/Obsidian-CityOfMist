#touched #henchmen
Touched veterans that serve [[Barton Canon, Avatar of the Spear|Barton, the spear]]. This seemingly harmless war touched men and women receive guidance of the spear through hallucinations and their weapons(which they normally have one that is their favorite). They can either recruit [[Sleepers|sleeper]] veterans who they know or manage or Barton can bring them together for temporary teams. Once the task is done they wont cross paths ever again.
