#Avatar #NPC #Beuty #Love #Nature 
Isabella is the [[Avatars|avatar]] of [[Xochiquetzal]], the Aztec Goddess of beauty; love; nature and birds. She want the world to be a green utopia where everyone can live at peace with nature. The problem? Humanity needs more than words to be convinced....
## Modus operandi
By the day Isabella uses her influence and powers to hypnotize the people of the city through her music. With her voice she can make it so people are more eager to care for plants to the point that they can value nature more than other humans. On the night she directs a bio-terrorist group called the Tamoanchan which she uses to attack greedy corporations that are harming the environment or get messages across slow moving politicians.
## The operation
Isabella's operation consist of two branches, the public and the terrorist. The former taking care of the public and the ladder takes care of companies and any one that needs a little more than words to stop harming nature.

## History
Isabella awakened to her mythos when she was a teenager.  She had a daughter that died in a protest to protect what is now the [[Arboretum]] and she became a tree that became the center of the enclave of Tamoanchan. Even as she was overcome with grief, Isabella was overwhelmed by the beauty of Tamoanchan. She could not escape the realization that true beauty requires the greatest sacrifice, a lesson she swore to impart to the inhabitants of the City.
