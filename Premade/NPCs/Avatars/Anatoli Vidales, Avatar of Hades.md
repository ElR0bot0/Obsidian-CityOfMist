## Custom moves
**Across the River Styx:** When you die, if you owed a debt to Death’s representatives or agreed to sell your soul in return for a second chance, you become a Living Dead. Remove the status that killed you. Take the permanent story tag <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">living dead</mark> and the ongoing status <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">lost-soul-1</mark>. At their option, servants of Death can incapacitate or kill you again, giving you a death-related tier-5 or tier-6 status of their choice. 

(For the MC: Borderliner servants of Anatoli can do so as a hard MC move, Legendaries can do so as a soft MC move, and Anatoli can do so as an MC intrusion).

**Ambrosia:** When you consume Ambrosia, if you have the story tag <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">living dead</mark> remove any <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">lost-soul</mark> or <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">fading-identity</mark> statuses you may have for the duration of the scene; restore them thereafter. If you don’t have the <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">living dead</mark> tag, choose one ongoing story tag to take: <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">increased stamina</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">uncanny strength</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">otherworldly beauty</mark>, or <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">irresistible charisma.</mark>

**Ambrosia Refinery:** If you have the story tag <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">living dead</mark> and you are drained of your memories in an Ambrosia refinery, tell everyone what was the memory you lost. You take <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">fading-identity-2</mark> and mark Crack on one of your Logos themes.
