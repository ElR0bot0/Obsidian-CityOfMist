## Custom moves
**Lover:** When you take a tier-5 infatuation status, you suddenly feel energized and positive about your new love affair. Take the temporary story tags <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">increased vigor</mark> and <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">throw caution to the wind</mark>. So long as you are involved with your love, you or the MC may have your character regain both these tags (together) at the start of a scene.

**Star-Crossed Lover:** Your careless infatuation takes a sudden turn for the worse. The MC can burn your <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">throw caution to the wind</mark> tag to make a hard move.
