## Custom moves
**Returning Customer:** When you buy a Chow Industries product, at the end of the next downtime, you crave more or better. Take <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">need-more-2</mark>, <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">want-upgrades-2</mark>, or <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">want-another-2</mark>. As long as you have this status, you continue to take the same tier-2 status at the end of every downtime. Once you get what you want, remove the status (although, if you fulfilled your desire through a Chow Industries product, this move applies anew)
