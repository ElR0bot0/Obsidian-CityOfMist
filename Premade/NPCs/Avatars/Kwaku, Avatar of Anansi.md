#Avatar #NPC #spiders #media #technology #trickery
Kwaku is the avatar of Anansi, the spider spirit of all stories. He want's to tell the best story possible but in his journey he wonders if he himself is the story, he only hopes know that its going to be a good one.
## Modus operandi
Basically, Kwaku has a network of informants and journalists called "[[webflo]]" and they will go around town, taking calls and covering and discovering new stories. Baku will then throw some complications or some help to make the story more interesting.
## The operation
Kwaku's operation has one main branch that collects information and engineer stories. It has a fluid and adaptive nature, like any good story.

## Custom moves
**The Lying Calabash:** Whenever you wish to take something out of the calabash or put something inside it, you must first **Sneak Around** and fool it. With every attempt the calabash adds to itself a permanent story tag that works against you (<mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">prank</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">trick</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">ruse</mark>, <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">stunt</mark>,  etc.). On a hit, you can choose one item to leave in the calabash or take one item out of it. If you take an item out, the MC will give you an appropriate permanent story tag, if relevant.

**The Lying Calabash, Part II:** The first time you take something out of the Lying Calabash, it’s a decoy that will only work once. When you first use the item, it works to some extent but then crumbles to dust. If you received a tag for this item, the tag expires after one invocation.

**The Lying Calabash, Part IV:** The third time you take something out of the Lying Calabash, you really got it.
