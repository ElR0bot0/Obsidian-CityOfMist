#avatar #NPC #war 
Barton is the [[Avatars|Avatar]] of the Spear, the collective desire of all leaders in history to win. His objective? To show his superiority in battle. The problem? to do so he first has to be losing.

## Modus operandi
Barton's modus operandi can be summed up to:
1. Find a conflict
2. join losing side
3. get them on equal level
4. leave and repeat with another confict
The spear's objective (at least for Barton) is to just have an objective, a challange, some way do demostrate his martial superiority. For the city is a little bit more complicated.

The spear wants the whole city to ensue in conflict and he does this by eciting it and equalising the forces. He can make two rival gangs start a war, or even just meddling with companies so that no one company is a monopoly that rules over the others.

## The operation
Barton's operation is impressive in its simplicity and complexity. The spear gives hallucinations to veteran operatives an they follow the seemingly random directions. They gather in public locations, meet each other, talk about combat experience, talk about hallucinations and sometimes they do so without even knowing they are part of something bigger. Sometimes Barton can even be between them without anyone knowing. This is helped by the mist's effect on the avatars that makes the memories of sleepers fade away at the presence of such an entity as an avatar.

## History 
At the age of 40, Barton is a relatively new Avatar. He suffered form depression and PTSD and tried to take his life. When he did so he awakened the Spear and there it started his thirst for victory. He was the second person to ever defeat [[Eddie Dreamtime]]. Even though he is a master in any combat he is so because he lost to the spear. Deep in Barton there is a man who just wants peace but had to lose that battle in order to win any other.

## Custom moves
War-Torn City: At the beginning of every session, the crew rolls+[tier of the City’s <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">war-torn</mark> status]. On 7-9, the MC chooses two. On 10+, the MC chooses three: 
- A major player or organization in the City is hit by an unknown party, becomes weakened, and then falls prey to its natural opposition. The City takes <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">war-torn-1</mark>.
- Rival organizations or gangs escalate to open war on the streets and innocent bystanders get caught up in the fight. The City takes <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">war-torn-1</mark>. 
- The police or military use unchecked force in order to restore the order, to no avail. The City takes <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">war-torn-1</mark>. 
- A financial giant suffers a great misfortune and collapses, bringing down the economy. People turn to looting and mugging to find food. The City takes <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">war-torn-1</mark>. 
- A downtrodden population successfully sues the City for compensation. Other under-privileged communities protest and all sides riot and clash on the streets. The City takes <mark style="background: #BBFABBA6;">war-torn-1</mark>. 

This status can only be reduced by appropriate actions that affect the entire city. At tier-5, the City is effectively in a state of inner war and is shut down. At tier-6 the City is irrevocably transformed or destroyed, MC’s choice. 


On a miss, your crew gains one Clue regarding the true instigators of these clashes.
