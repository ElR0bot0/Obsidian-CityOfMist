If the crew already came upon the cult’s artifacts during The Furnace (page 87), such as the Tincture of the Gingerbread Witch or the Rift-harvesting furnace itself, or if they had a run-in with the cult’s Disciples, they may already be looking for answers about the cult’s strange magic. Researching the secret art of the cultists can lead to answers ([[Alchemy 101]]). Seeking out the cult’s operation will initially lead to a dead end or to an ambush ([[The Cabal’s Damage Control]]).
