# Background

When a PC or a supporting cast character who has consumed Candy starts craving more, they begin to hallucinate a trail of glowing blue pebbles that leads them into the night (even if it’s daytime). Following the trail leads the Candy user through an urban forest (back alleys, subway tunnels, rooftops, canals, etc.) to [[The Aurora Glassworks old Factory|the old Aurora Glassworks factory]]. During this time, the Candy user is moonstruck (at your option, **moonstruck-3**) and only half aware of what is happening around her.

As the character is making her journey to the Candy factory, you can set the scene by narrating or ask the player to narrate what the character is feeling and thinking about her wishes and what was it like for her to see them fulfilled and then fade away
