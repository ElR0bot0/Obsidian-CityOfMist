# Background
The Candy drug lab is situated in a converted basement space of a textile factory in the Industrial Zone. It is operated by the Underhand Naga Gang, an Asian ethnic street gang of mixed Cambodian, Vietnamese, Thai, and Chinese origins. Despite its mythic name, the gang members are all Sleepers, although a handful of the lab workers who have had long exposure to Candy have become Touched by it.

This is a good location for a suspenseful scene, if the crew is sneaking in, or an action scene, if they charge in or if things go south. Investigating this location allows the crew to complete the picture about Candy manufacturing, pointing them at the source of the drug.
# Clues
- The gang picks up a vat of pure Candy once a week at the loading dock of the old Aurora Glassworks factory. There is no one else there during pickup. There is no evidence of the gang paying for the drug.
- The gang processes the drug here and sells it via the network of drug dealer Foxy Supreme
- The operation was set up with Leckermaul while she was still around. A month ago the gang members found an oblivious Leckermaul cuffed to the railing next to the vat of Candy with a note saying “business as usual”. They took her to her friend, Foxy Supreme.
- Obtaining pure Candy makes it easier to study its properties (see under Candy, page 90).
