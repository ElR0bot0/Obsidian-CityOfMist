# Background
The scene at Will Harris’ modest one-bedroom apartment can play out in two ways:
- Will Harris may still be missing, in which case her 20-something-year-old sister, Beth (Sleeper), is there watching Will’s son, Albert (Sleeper). Both of them are quite distraught by the situation and seem to be lost without Will’s leadership.
- Alternatively, Will could have returned home in the last few hours, having gone through the furnace and lost her powers. She is a tall and lithe woman in her 30s, sporting a funky crew cut and casual jeans and T-shirt. She seems washed out and distant.
This is mostly an investigative location; NPCs here are non-violent Sleepers and are unlikely to cause a fuss unless the PCs push them into a corner, in which case the police might be called.
# Clues
## Investigating the Disappearance
- Will Harris has been (or was) missing for the last day or so. She was last seen yesterday morning dropping off Albert at kindergarten, but she didn’t show up to pick him up, which never happened before. The kindergarten called Beth as backup. Later, Beth found out Will didn’t show up to work that day (she works as a graphic designer for a non-profit a few blocks away)
- Will was struggling to pay the bills and then recently had a windfall that allowed her to pay off all her bills. This can be revealed by chatting to Beth, tracking Will’s bank account history, rummaging in the dustbin, or gossiping with the landlady downstairs.
- The windfall money seems to have come out of nowhere, e.g., she deposited a large sum of cash or it just appeared in her account without a sender.
- Both Beth and Albert, each in their own way, know that Will was leading a secret life: she wasn’t always frank about her nightly outings twice a week. Beth once followed her to a rave in the Industrial Zone, but wasn’t allowed in ([[The Bowl fight club]]). They worry that this has something to do with her windfall and subsequent disappearance.
- Behind the wardrobe, someone punched a hole in the wall and stashed some items: three badass crossbows, both antique and modern, and half a dozen empty Candy wrappers.
- Albert is afraid that he had a part in his mom’s disappearance. He says a kid from school, Charlie, made him steal some candy from his mom’s jacket pocket a few days before she disappeared. He also says Charlie has a gigantic toy room, bigger than this whole building ([[Charlie Littles' toy room]]).
- Will got her Candy from Carter ([[The Bowl fight club]]).
## Investigating the return
- Will behaves normally, although she seems tired and somewhat detached. However both Beth and Albert are alarmed at Will’s behavior. According to them, Will was always upbeat and witty.
- Will is a Sleeper; that much is evident in a conversation and can be confirmed beyond the Mist. She has no memory of being a Rift and only vaguely remembers going to raves in the Industrial Zone and doing drugs, a troubled period that she’s now adamant to leave behind her.
- **Further beyond the Mist**, Will has the furnace trace (see The Furnace Trace sidebar, page 93).
