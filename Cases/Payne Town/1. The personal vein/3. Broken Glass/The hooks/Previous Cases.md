The crew may continue the investigation from previous cases, Killing Her Softly and Carnival of Machines, where the villains are both out-ofcontrol Rifts who came into contact with Mending the Broken therapists. Whether or not the crew believes their claims that their therapists drove them to do harm, they may wish to investigate this lead by locating Martha Ellis’ therapist Dr. Selma Perrin or Maximillian Drake’s therapist Dr. Archibald Tennant. Both therapists operate their own private clinics which would be easy to find. The crew may also investigate the Mending the Broken organization in general (see private clinic).
