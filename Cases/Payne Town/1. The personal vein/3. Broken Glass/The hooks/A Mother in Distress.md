If you are playing this case as a one-shot, or if you want to add additional scenes, the crew can be called to help with a domestic violence case which will point them to Mending the Broken. Felicia Pema Jones (Palden Lhamo, wrathful protector goddess of Tibet and its spiritual leaders) is a young Rift mother who has become depressed and violent after giving birth. Her husband, Jeff, either called social services or the police for assistance with his wife, or, thinking her possessed, asked around and reached the crew. Following this lead has the crew starting at the Joneses’ apartment.
