# Background
This scene serves as an introduction to Whitecliff and the current situation therein, as well as laying the ground for a later plot twist regarding Cyrus/Beaumont.

Outwardly, the Concerned Citizens Council is a volunteer body that organizes the Whitecliff residents’ efforts to improve their community, mostly by preventing crime. They run a neighborhood watch program to help police patrols, and at the same time maintain deep connections with local gangsters in order to de-escalate situations involving residents and their property. Despite their best efforts, more and more of Whitecliff is falling prey to gangs and mobs, which they ascribe to Mayor Ricci’s incompetence (but in truth is due to the fact that the mundane police are unable to stop Rift gangs).

Behind the scenes, the CCC is the incubator for the resurgence of the Arthur Mythos and with it, the memories and violent psyche of Vince Beaumont. The Mythos reawakened in the head of the CCC, Calvin Cyrus, who, like King Arthur, was fighting the invasion of foreign forces into his beloved neighborhood of Whitecliff. Layered with both the Arthur Mythos and Beaumont’s knowledge and rage against the Crimson King, Cyrus/Beaumont now plans to make Whitecliff the strongest neighborhood in the City and the platform from which he will launch his final attack on his old archnemesis. To that end, he has been secretly operating the Errants gang

## CYRUS IN THIS DEPTH
Cyrus/Beaumont is a clever man empowered by a legendary wise king; he won’t expose himself this early in the game. Using an Avalonian Charm to hide his Rifthood from other Rifts (provided by Céline Rowland, Rift of the Lady of the Lake, see Céline Rowland's Lake House), he pretends to be just Calvin Cyrus, a concerned resident who wants the gangs out of Whitecliff. That much is true, so Calvin isn’t lying as such, but sharp PCs can detect that his intentions go beyond just cleaning up the streets.

Calvin Cyrus will act as expected of the mild-mannered and gingerly head of the CCC. He will pretend to be completely supportive of the PCs’ actions to solve the situation, even if they go against the Errants (as long as they don’t hurt the residents of the neighborhood). In this Depth, he is simply weighing them in his palm, observing them and judging whether to recruit, and how. He also wants them to eventually find out about him, so he can reveal his plan – but not yet.

When a PC Investigates Calvin Cyrus, especially if they use Mythos power tags, use the counter question option to have him collect information about their abilities and motivations, without indicating who is collecting the information.

If Calvin Cyrus invited the crew here in Depth 0, he will give them the first and/or the second clue below to start them on their investigation.

# Clues
- There is a new biker gang fighting for turf in Whitecliff, called the Errants. They are hard to identify because they wear motorcycle helmets. They have hit some local businesses, like the [[Double Dragon chinese restaurant]], causing some property damage and sowing fear.
- Word on the street is the Errants are cleaning out Whitecliff. A showdown with one of the oldest gangs in Whitecliff, the Skoonies, is set to take place tonight at a chosen [[The Basketball Court|basketball court]].
- Gangs have always preyed on Whitecliff due to its central location in this part of the City. Unfortunately, Mayor Ricci and the local Precinct 63 have been unsuccessful in stopping crime in the neighborhood.
- The CCC is just a volunteer body of residents trying to help Mayor Ricci and the police, but because they want what’s best for the residents, they often work things out directly with the gangs.
## THE CCC VOLUNTEERS
The volunteers are **busy-2**, making it difficult to have a decent conversation with them.
- There is no love lost between the CCC and the mayor’s office. Most volunteers seem disillusioned with what the system has done or even can do for Whitecliff.
- Volunteer Rita Morales walks in wearing a straw hat and holding a pitchfork and a sack of turnips. She had just come in from working in [[The Community Gardens|the community garden]] and has soil under her nails. She seems elated and will attest to the positive, wholesome experience she has when she goes there.
- Volunteer Chuck Gorski thinks the Errants are doing the neighborhood a favor, accomplishing in a month more than the police ever did. Based on your cinematic considerations, this may be empty talk or he could be echoing his friends in the Errants and may lead the crew to them (see next clue).
- Checking the computers or work tickets, some of the volunteers, mostly the younger ones, haven’t clocked in the last two weeks (they have joined the Errants). Tracking them down could lead to [[the basketball court]], [[the highway barricades]], or [[the junkyard]], eventually proving the Errants may be an offshoot of the CCC.
- Similarly, analyzing the neighborhood watch program run by the CCC shows they were coincidentally – or conveniently – not patrolling the locations that the Errants’ hit at the time the gang was there, hinting at a possible mole in the CCC.
## CALVIN CYRUS
- Asking around about Calvin Cyrus reveals just how much the neighborhood loves him for his work and how much he loves the neighborhood. A middle-aged man, he has been living in Whitecliff for 25 years, having moved here from the suburbs. There is nothing out of the ordinary in his records, then or now.
- Following Calvin or tapping his calls may reveal he is in touch with the Errants. If confronted before he is ready to reveal himself, he will say it’s a part of his job to foster backchannels with the gangs.
- Beyond the Mist, everyone here is a Sleeper enshrouded in Mist including Calvin Cyrus. If the crew is stuck, you can drop a hint that the Mist around him seems different, but that he still appears to be a Sleeper. This can be confirmed by reading his reaction when he is asked whether he witnessed anything legendary, which will force him to come out or lie. Only a Significant Stop. Holding. Back. move can break the Avalonian Charm masking his Rifthood.
