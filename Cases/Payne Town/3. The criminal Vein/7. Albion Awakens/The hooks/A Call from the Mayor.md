If the crew is known to solve problems of the criminal kind or is otherwise available for contract work, Mayor Ricci could contact them discreetly and ask for their help with figuring out who is behind the latest crime wave in Whitecliff. The crew can talk to her at her office, situated at the heart of Whitecliff and overlooking Precinct 63, or on the sidelines of a political rally or a police awards ceremony held in a park, just as she’s about to give a speech and try to reassure the anxious citizens of the neighborhood. The mayor is willing to pay a considerable fee to anyone who could find and bring to justice the leader of the new gang that has been intimidating local businesses. She’ll point the crew to one of the affected businesses, the [[Double Dragon chinese restaurant]].
