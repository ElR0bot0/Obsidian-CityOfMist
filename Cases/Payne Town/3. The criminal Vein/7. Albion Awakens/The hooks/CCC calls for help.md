Calvin Cyrus (Beaumont) could call the crew himself, if he’s thinking of recruiting them. This is especially relevant if one of the crew has a reputation in the Rift underworld of wielding a legendary weapon that could help bring down the Crimson King or another reputed ability that Beaumont covets for his march on Precinct 63. PCs with Mythoi of knights or other medieval connections also attract his Mythos. Sounding upset and saddened on the phone, he will tell the crew that the [[Council of Concerned Citizens|Concerned Citizens Committee]] needs their help in bringing order and safety back to the streets of Whitecliff and invite them to have a longer chat at the CCC offices.
