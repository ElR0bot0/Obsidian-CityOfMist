Crew members monitoring crime and gang activity will receive reports of a turf war in Whitecliff. Underworld informants can share that a new biker gang, styling themselves as the Errants, have moved in on Whitecliff and pushed out several smaller local gangs, taking over the neighborhood block by block. They are currently fighting one of the oldest gangs in the district, the Skoonies, who are known for their stealthy guerilla tactics. Word on the street is that the Skoonies have entrenched themselves in the local sewers and that the Errants are having trouble rooting them out. The gangs have agreed to settle the issue tonight at [[the basketball court]].
