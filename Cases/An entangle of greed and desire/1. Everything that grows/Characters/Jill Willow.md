## What is the Mythos of the main Rift(s) involved?
Jack sparrow
## Who is this Rift in her everyday life in the City?
A doctor
## How and when did this Rift awaken?
When she was a highly acclaimed experimental doctor she lost a patient and ended up being degraded. Now she works at a dead end consulting room. After loosing everything dear to her, her mythos appeared, driving her to achieve greatness at the cost of anyone that got in her way... and also those that didn't.
## How awakened is this Rift?
<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Legendary</mark>
## What drives this Rift more: her Mythos or her ordinary life?
Both
## What does her Mythos want from her?
To achieve greatness and become the slyest person alive.
## What is / was her mundane motive?
To become the best doctor there is.
## How did she use her Mythos powers to achieve her goal?
By using her mythos, she gets powers of divination in the form of knowing what people want and subversion to actually convince them. This makes her a master manipulator.
