# Backstory
## What Happened before the crew got involved in the case?
Well, I do have the idea of jack sparrow being the one in the center of this all, I had the idea that her plan is to make a bombing in order to injury people so she would have a lot of patients, enough to get back her previous Hospital (Her black pearl). Now, I wanted it to be a opposing force in the story, someone that wants to prevent this from happening and is framed as a villain early in the investigation.

What is happening in the background is that Jill was tasked with making a cure for the Dryad prototype, so far she has been doing experiments on her patients without them knowing whenever they come for a regular check in. This has caused a estrange mutation on the patients that makes them develop strange disease a moss like sprout across the skin and ends up making them ravage and become crazy. This made a case of police brutality take place, as one of the affected lost control of themselves and tried to attack an officer.  The crew will have to piece the clues together before the terrorist attack takes place.
