
# Backstory
## What Happened before the crew got involved in the case?
	[[Eleanor Ashwell]] is a construction worker that works everyday in hopes of one day having enough money to raise a family. Unfortunately, the paychecks are barely enough to sustain herself as she saves up and she also has no one to share this dream with. One day she some crocs where sitting on her window, almost calling to them she put them on. The crocs changed her whole look into her own ideal version. Eventually they guided her to a shop where she would find a potion maker that made her a potion of love and said that she would find her true love this way. By fate she stumbled into a great ball when the shoes made it so she looked the same as a childhood friend of hers. The ball was the celebration of a wedding and in confusion the groom thought she was the bride and decided to get her to the upper room. This was seen by many people who answered to the bride when she was asking for her groom. The clock ringed midnight as she returned to her old self, making it so the groom knew who she was. The bride catched them and a whole drama has spurred out of this. 2 months have gone by since this has happened.
