## THE SERIES CONCEPT
>  The series concept, established at the beginning of the Exposition Session, defines what your group (yourself included) would like the series to be about. The nature of the crew, its goals, and its modus operandi form the baseline from which everything in the series arises. Most if not all of the cases in your series should be written in accordance with the series concept, or adapted to it, so keep it in mind.

Past amends cleaning company is our series, is about a group of misfits that have trouble in their lives and in their past that they don't want to gent into an and paradoxically they stray away from by resolving other people's problems. The problem is that there problems are going to get them eventually.
